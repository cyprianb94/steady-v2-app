Today card V4

A calmer version of the familiar card. Before-run states are one instruction, not a grid: session chip top left, Today top right, a single target frame, and one quiet detail line only when the workout needs it.

What changed

This keeps the familiar Steady card shape, but removes the before-run dashboard feeling. The card says what to run; comparison waits until after.

Chip placement
Session type is always top left.

Before runs use Today top right. After runs use Completed or Needs review top right.

Before the run
One target frame, not three fields.

Easy and long lead with effort. Tempo and interval lead with the quality pace, with warm-up and recovery collapsed to one line.

After the run
Keep the stronger logged state.

Logged cards lead with a verdict and a slim evidence list. Heart rate only appears later if it explains that verdict.

Session colour: border, chip, session title.
Clay: Today and primary action.
Cobalt: distance values.
Teal: pace targets and results.
Plum: effort and feel.
