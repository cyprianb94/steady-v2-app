Shared Blocks

Lightweight sharing for training blocks. A club, coach, or runner shares an unlisted link; another runner previews the block and imports an editable copy. No marketplace, no paywall, no live subscription to someone else's plan.

Product direction

Sharing, not a marketplace.

The first version should prove whether trusted links drive adoption before Steady invests in public discovery.

Free principle

Any runner can create, share, import, fork, and follow a block for free.

Distribution loop

QR code at a club run, Instagram link, WhatsApp post, or coach handoff.

Technical constraint

Imported blocks are snapshots. The runner owns their editable copy.

Paid bridge

Steady AI can adapt a shared block around missed sessions, recent training, or injury.
