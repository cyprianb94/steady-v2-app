1. Block draws The phase strip fills left to right and the current-week marker drops into place.
2. Runs appear The planned and actual trace draws over a small timing-card surface.
3. Week stamps in Three compact session rows land with a controlled stopwatch-detent rhythm.
4. CTA settles The copy and primary action stay still while the product preview does the work.
